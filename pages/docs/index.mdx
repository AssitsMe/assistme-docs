# Introduction


Welcome to the AssistMe platform! Our platform offers unique capabilities for creating AI Copilots for your business, significantly enhancing your productivity and efficiency. Let's go through the main steps for a quick start.

## Platform Overview

Get to know the main features and benefits of the AssistMe platform, including our unique search algorithm and the built-in document search mechanism.

### Unique Features

- **Reduced Hallucinations:** Our algorithm minimizes errors and false information.
- **No-Code:** Easily create powerful AI agents without needing deep technical knowledge.
- **Advanced Document Search Mechanism:** Integrate and search your documents directly through the AI Copilot.
