# Telegram Inbox

This page guides you through setting up the telegram inbox

## How to setup a Telegram channel?

Step 1. Go to Engagement Hub → Settings → Inboxes → “Add Inbox”.

<Frame>![telegram Step 1](/images/hub/telegramStep1.png)</Frame>

Step 2. Click on the "Telegram" icon.

<Frame>![telegram Step 2](/images/hub/telegramStep2.png)</Frame>

Step 3. Create a new telegram bot using Telegram [BotFather](https://telegram.me/BotFather).

Step 4. Enter the API token of the Telegram bot and click on "Create Telegram Channel".

<Frame>![telegram Step 3](/images/hub/telegramStep3.png)</Frame>

Step 5. "Add operators" to your Telegram inbox.

<Frame>![telegram Step 2](/images/hub/telegramStep4.png)</Frame>

Step 6. "Add agents" to your Telegram inbox.

<Frame>![telegram Step 2](/images/hub/telegramStep5.png)</Frame>

The inbox setup is complete.

Step 7. Send a message to the Telegram bot. Check Telegram inbox for the new message.

<Frame>![telegram Step 2](/images/hub/telegramStep6.png)</Frame>
